UNIVERSITY PROFILE

The Konark University Act was passed by the Odisha Legislature on 10th December, 1966 to fulfill long cherished dream of the people of Odisha for establishment of a University. The University started functioning from 1st January, 1967 with Prof.Parsura Mishra as the first Vice-Chancellor.
